# Web3 Essentials: What You Need to Know

Web3 is reshaping how we interact with the internet. Here are the essentials:

- **Decentralization**: Power shifts from centralized entities to users.
- **Blockchain technology**: The backbone of Web3, enabling trustless transactions.
- **Smart contracts**: Automate agreements and reduce the need for intermediaries.
- **Digital ownership**: NFTs and tokens empower users to own digital assets.

## Why Web3 Matters

Web3 promises a more open, user-centric internet. Understanding its fundamentals is key to staying ahead in the digital age.
